Welcome to EnguiMapCLI

EnguiMapCLI was created as the result of a partnership between EnguiSoft and Kraig Beahn, CEO at L2Networks Corp.

EnguiMapCLI provides basic CLI style IP MTU testing, estimating the default internet transit providers maximum transport unit, while also providing standard network adapter information, including the hardware (mac-address), assigned IPv4 address, gateway status and round trip time in a convenient, yet compact and easy to read format.

Written in both Pure C, AutoIT v3 and wrapped into a single, portable executable for easy transfer and use.

This tool is being provided to the global Information Technology community, in hopes that it may be useful in streamlining local network support and in turn spurring a more detailed knowledge of how Layer-2 and Layer-3 technology functions, at a more advanced, enterprise level.
